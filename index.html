Intro

This Discord server has been created with the aim of becoming the go-to place for all that is Riot Games. Starting off small with less that 100 members, we have built this server from the ground up!

In the future we hope to incoporate all Riot Games, including mobile games and the much anticapated MMORPG that is currently under development. Information on this new MMORPG can be found Here!

Goals

As mentioned in the Intro section, we plan on becoming the go-to place for all that is Riot Games. The server will primarily focus on Riot's main games.

  • LEAGUE OF LEGENDS
  • VALORANT
  • TEAMFIGHT TACTICS
  • LEGENDS OF RUNETERRA
  • RUINED KING

The mobile games may be added in the future, depending on the servers playerbase of these games.

  • LOL: WILDRIFT
  • HEXTECH MAYHEM

All games available will have text channels, media channels and voice channels. Here you can chat and share with other members playing the same games!
